Work Examples

Here are just a few of my Projects that I have completed over my time as a developer. I am involved in quite a bit of contracted work but I am able to demo some of my applications upon request.

Label Creation & Management App

A full fledged label creation and mail tracking application designed for companies and organizations dealing with large amounts of mail creation. Creates USPS Compliant Labels for both Certified and Confirmation mail. Automates tracking of created labels using the USPS API, archiving information for much longer than the USPS standard.

Birthday Bot

Birthday Bot is a Slack bot application written in PHP that announces birthdays for the Digital Corps. It also comes with slash commands that are able to provide users with the birthdays in a month or with a Corps member's birthday.

Edwards Rentals
